About Mohd Aatif AI

Mohd Aatif AI is an advanced AI assistant and educational tool designed to provide users with:

  • Islamic Duas for daily activities, prayer, fasting, and Ramadan.
  • General knowledge, calculator, and helpful online resources.
  • Instant answers and links for any question typed by the user.

This AI assistant is developed by Mohd Aatif with a focus on providing a professional and user-friendly experience. Currently, it is in beta version, and new features will be added over time.

Professionally designed to be monetization-friendly, responsive, and fully functional on desktop and mobile.
